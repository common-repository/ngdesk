== Description ==

ngDesk chat app that lets you easily chat with customers on your website with our easy to install plugin. No catch. No spam. No Ads. It's truly free and always will be.

* Gain valuable insight when you monitor your website visitors in real time.
* Stay connected anywhere, be in touch with your customers from your computer, or your mobile.
* Live Chat is convenient for your customers, helping them through pain points and as a result helping you generate better customer support and higher conversions.

ngDesk is designed to increase the effectiveness in managing the online customer engagement experience, enabling multiple websites and agents in a single dashboard interface to chat with the visitors on your website.

ngDesk is also available for mobile. Visit the Google Play Store or Apple App Store to download the app on your phone.

== Installation ==

Adding ngdesk live chat widget to your Wordpress site is really easy, follow these steps :

* Clone this repository into your plugins directory or download the zip file and unzip on your plugins directory
* or Install the Free plugin from the WordPress directory and activate it.
* Go to `Settings`, then to the `ngDesk` sub menu, to select a widget.
* Customize the chat widget from within the [ngDesk company settings] to your preference.
* Start chatting with your visitors! :)
